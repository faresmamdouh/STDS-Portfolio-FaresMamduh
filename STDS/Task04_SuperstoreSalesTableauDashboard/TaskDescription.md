# Task 04: Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Task Overview
This task focuses on analyzing sales performance and profitability using the Superstore dataset.  
An interactive Tableau dashboard was developed to explore sales trends, regional performance, and product profitability.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing and underperforming regions
- Evaluate product and sub-category profitability
- Explore sales trends over time
- Support business decision-making through visual analytics

## Tools & Technologies
- Tableau
- Data Visualization
- Calculated Fields
- Filters and Parameters
- Dashboard Design

## Dataset
The Sample Superstore dataset includes:
- Orders and sales transactions
- Profit and discount information
- Product categories and sub-categories
- Customer segments
- Geographic data (region, state, city)
- Order and ship dates
