# Task 02: AdventureWorks Sales & Product Analytics Dashboard

## Task Overview
This task focuses on analyzing sales performance, profitability, and inventory insights using the AdventureWorks dataset.  
The objective is to design interactive dashboards that provide both high-level sales summaries and detailed product-level insights.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales trends across years and months
- Evaluate profitability and revenue contribution by product category
- Identify top-performing products by profit margin and inventory volume
- Analyze geographic sales distribution
- Support business decision-making through visual analytics

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
- KPI Cards
- Slicers (Year, Month, Country, Category)
- Dashboard Design Techniques

## Dataset
AdventureWorks sales data including:
- Orders and revenue
- Profit and profit margin
- Product categories and subcategories
- Inventory levels
- Geographic markets
