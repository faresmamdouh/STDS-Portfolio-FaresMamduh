# Task 03: LogiPro Distributorsa Operations Dashboard

## Task Overview
This task focuses on analyzing sales operations, inventory management, and supplier performance for LogiPro Distributorsa.  
The objective is to build interactive dashboards that provide executive-level insights as well as operational and supply chain analytics.

## Objectives
- Monitor overall sales and order performance
- Track inventory turnover and stock status
- Analyze warehouse reorder requirements
- Evaluate supplier performance and delivery reliability
- Support data-driven operational decisions

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX Measures
- Interactive Visualizations
- Filters and Slicers

## Dataset
Operational and transactional data including:
- Sales and orders
- Inventory stock levels and reorder points
- Warehouses and regions
- Supplier delivery performance
- Lead time and on-time delivery metrics
